My Hobby

My favorite thing to do on the weekend
is to go to the track. NOT the running track but
riding track where you can ride quads and four wheelers.
My dad usually takes us to Rout 62 MX track, because our friends own it
and we can get in for free. Im the "number girl" there. which
is the girl that goes out and starts the race. I like to ride too.
I havn't learned how to ride a dirtbike yet but I really
want to. My dad won't let me because my brother just learned how to ride
one and I learn faster than he does, so he will get mad if I can ride better than him.

More about Riding

Riding is like the funnest thing I have ever done.
When we go to Route 62 there are so many people that I know now because
I go there a lot. I don't even know where to begin with the
amount of cute boys there.Litteraly if your looking for
a boyfriend go there.
When I go there in the summer time I like to get there supper early in the morning
when nobody else is there. So I can go crazy and ride my quad super fast down
gravel road. Since we know the people that own Route 62 we can basically do whatever we want.
they have this big barn with an indoor track for the winter time that we are allowed to go in.
And nobody else is. Ugh, I just can't describe and explain how awsome it is there.

Memories at the Track

I have so many fun memories at the track that I can't forget.
One time my friend Beau(also my brother Gavins friend, and the owners son)
dirtbike caught on fire. I didn't see it personaly but I could see the
mark on the gravel from where it caught on fire. Another great memeory I
is when the first time I ever went there. I didn't know that Beaus parents
owned the track. My brother wanted to spend the night at his house so Beaus mom told us to come to the track to drop him
off because they where still there. When we got there it was amazing. I was so happy.
So we dropped off my brother and Jimmy and Kristen, Beau and Cadens
parents, asked us to come to the track the next day(saturday).And we did.

Why I Like Riding

I think because my dad always rode when he was a kid
it got me and my brothers hooked to it.My dad used to tell me and my brothers Stories
about him and his best friend. My dad and his best friend(Chris)are still
friends till this day. They lived close to eachother when they where kids and used to ride quads through
the fields to get to eachothers houses. I think it's cool how they are still bestest friends.
My dad used to have a dirtbike but then he got in an accident and got rid of it.
Then he got a racing quad. He still has the racing quad. I think another reason
I'm addicted to riding is that all of my dads friends have quads. When my dads old friend used
to live in our neiborhood we would go over to his house whenever we wanted. They where like a second family to us.
Then all our other friends that lived in the neiborhood came over with their quads, and it was like
a big quad party. One more reason I think I like riding is that when I'm at my dads house I'm with all boys.
The boys always like to do boy stuff, so I always got dragged into it.
I think I just got used to the idea of riding an then I started to like it because we did it all the time.
